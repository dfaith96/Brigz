Privacy Policy

This demo policy explains how Brigz may collect, use, and protect information submitted through the website. Review it with a privacy professional before using it for a real company.

1. Information we collect

Brigz may collect contact details such as your name, work email, company, and message when you submit a demo request. If a product is used, Brigz may also process survey responses, feedback, usage events, and workspace settings.

2. How we use information

We use information to respond to demo requests, provide product information, improve the website, support customers, and understand how product teams use Brigz.

3. Customer feedback data

Feedback and research data should be used to generate insights for the customer who collected it. Brigz should only process personal data according to the permissions, notices, and agreements that apply to each workspace.

4. Sharing information

Brigz does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the website, manage demo requests, host data, or provide support.

5. Security

Brigz uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No website or online service can guarantee complete security.

6. Your choices

You may contact Brigz to request access, correction, or deletion of information you submitted through the website, subject to applicable legal and business requirements.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@brigz.space.
